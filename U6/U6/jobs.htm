Cloud Engineer: A cloud engineer specialises in designing, implementing, and managing cloud-based solutions, leveraging platforms like Google Cloud. They focus on optimising infrastructure, enhancing scalability, and ensuring security and compliance. To become a cloud engineer you will need 4-5 GCSEs at grade A*-C.

Data Quality Manager: A data quality manager oversees the integrity and reliability of data within an organization. They establish and enforce data quality standards, implement processes for data cleansing and validation, and ensure compliance with regulations. Data quality managers play a critical role in enabling data-driven decision-making and maintaining trust in organisational data assets. To become a data quality manager you will need 4-5 GCSEs at grade A*-C.

Systems Analyst: A systems analyst bridges business needs with technological solutions, analysing systems, proposing improvements, and ensuring alignment with business objectives. They play a vital role in the software development lifecycle, from requirements gathering to implementation. To become a system analysist you will need 4-5 GCSEs at grade A*-C.

Web Developer: Web developers design and build websites and web applications using programming languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They collaborate with designers to create user-friendly websites that are visually appealing, responsive, and optimised for performance. To become a web developer you will need 4-5 GCSEs at grade A*-C.
